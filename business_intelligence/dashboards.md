## Dashboards (Mockups)

### Executive Summary
- **KPIs**: Total Appointments (MTD), Completion Rate, No-Show Rate, Top 5 Doctors by Load
- **Charts**: Weekly appointment trend, Monthly prescription volume
- **Tables**: Department utilization ranking

### Audit Dashboard
- **Cards**: Denied Operations Today, Denied Last 7 Days
- **Charts**: Denials by table (bar), Denials over time (line)
- **Table**: Latest 50 audit entries with user, table, operation, status

### Performance Dashboard
- **KPIs**: Avg Appointments per Doctor per Day, Follow-Up adherence
- **Charts**: Patient visit cadence (time between visits), Doctor utilization trend
- **Tables**: Appointment load by doctor/department

- Use `queries/analytics_queries.sql` and `queries/audit_queries.sql` for data sources.
