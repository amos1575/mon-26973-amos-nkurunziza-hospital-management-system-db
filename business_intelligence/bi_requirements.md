## BI Requirements

- **Stakeholders**: Hospital Admin, Department Heads, Doctors, Pharmacy, Finance
- **Decision Support Needs**:
  - **Appointment Utilization**: busiest departments/doctors, weekly trends
  - **Patient Flow**: visit frequency, no-show rates, follow-up adherence
  - **Medication Insights**: top prescribed meds, stock-out risk
  - **Performance**: average waiting time, doctor utilization
  - **Compliance**: audit denials, restriction violations
- **Reporting Frequency**:
  - **Operational**: Daily summaries
  - **Tactical**: Weekly department reports
  - **Executive**: Monthly KPIs and trends
- **Data Sources**: `appointments`, `patients`, `doctors`, `medical_records`, `prescriptions`, `audit_logs`, `public_holidays`
